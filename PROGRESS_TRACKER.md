## 💡 Tips for Success

1. **Consistency > Intensity:** Study 1 hour daily rather than 10 hours once a week
2. **Active Learning:** Code along, don't just read
3. **Build Projects:** Apply concepts immediately
4. **Share Progress:** Post on LinkedIn, Twitter
5. **Help Others:** Teaching reinforces learning
6. **Take Breaks:** Rest is part of learning
7. **Celebrate Wins:** Acknowledge your progress!
